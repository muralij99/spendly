Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filtering. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to you when logged in. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. No expense data or personally identifiable information is shared with any third party. We do not integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers.
